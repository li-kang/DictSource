chic·ory / ˈtʃɪkəri ; NAmE ˈtʃɪkəri / noun [uncountable ] 1 ( BrE) ( NAmE en·dive ) [countable ,  uncountable ] a small pale green plant with bitter leaves that are eaten raw or cooked as a vegetable.
