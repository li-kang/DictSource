reˈduce sb/sth (from sth) to sth/to doing sth
[usually passive]to force sb/sth into a particular state or condition, usually a worse one使陷入（更坏的）境地；使沦落；使陷入窘境a beautiful building reduced to rubble已化为残垣断壁的漂亮建筑She wasreduced to tearsby their criticisms.他们的批评使她流下了眼泪。They were reduced to begging in the streets.他们沦落到沿街乞讨。
