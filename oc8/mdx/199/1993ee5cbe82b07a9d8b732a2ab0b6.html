你能否给我举例说明一下发生这事的情况？ cite or quote? 用 cite 还是 quote？ You can citereasons or examples, but you can only quoteexamples. *cite 可指提及原因或举出示例，但 quote 只用于举例： ◆ He quoted his heavy workload as the reason for his breakdown.
