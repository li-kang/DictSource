eh / eɪ ; NAmE eɪ / exclamation ( BrE) ( NAmE usually huh ) 1 the sound that people make when they want sb to repeat sth （请对方再说一遍）嗯，什么 ◆ ‘I'm not hungry.’ ‘Eh?’ ‘I said I'm not hungry.’ “我不饿。”“嗯？”“我说我不饿。” 2 the sound that people make when they want sb to agree or reply （征求对方同意或答覆）是吗，好吗，嗯 ◆ So what do you think, eh? 那你是怎么想的，嗯？ 3 the sound people make when they are surprised （表示惊奇）啊 ◆ Another new dress, eh! 呵，又是一件新连衣裙！ eh / eɪ ; NAmE eɪ /
