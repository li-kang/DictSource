hyper·cor·rec·tion / ˌhaɪpəkəˈrekʃn ; NAmE ˌhaɪpərkəˈrekʃn / noun [uncountable ,  countable ] ( linguistics 语言 ) the use of a wrong form or pronunciation of a word by sb who is trying to show that they can use language correctly.
