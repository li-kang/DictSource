there·abouts / ˌðeərəˈbaʊts ; NAmE ˌðerəˈbaʊts / adverb ( usually used after or 通常用于 or 之后 ) 1 near the place mentioned 在那附近 ◆ He comes from Leeds or thereabouts. 他是利兹或其附近的人。 2 used to say that a particular number, quantity, time, etc. is not exact （数量、时间等）大约，左右，上下 ◆ They paid $100 000 or thereabouts for the house. 他们买那房子花了大约 10 万元。 IDIOMsee there   adv. there·abouts / ˌðeərəˈbaʊts ; NAmE ˌðerəˈbaʊts /
