be in two ˈminds about sth/about doing sth
(BrE)(NAmEbe of two ˈminds about sth/about doing sth)to be unable to decide what you think about sb/sth, or whether to do sth or not犹豫不决；拿不定主意I was in two minds about the book(= I didn't know if I liked it or not).我说不清我是否喜欢这本书。She's in two minds about accepting his invitation.是否接受他的邀请，她犹豫不决。
