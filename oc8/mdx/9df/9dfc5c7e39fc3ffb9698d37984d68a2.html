bil·lion ★ / ˈbɪljən ; NAmE ˈbɪljən / number ( plural verb 复数动词 ) 1 ★ ( abbr.bn ) 1 000 000 000; one thousand million 十亿 ◆ Worldwide sales reached 2.5 billion. 全球销售额达到了 25 亿。 ◆ half a billion dollars 五亿元 ◆ They have spent billions on the problem (= billions of dollars, etc.). 他们花了几十亿元解决这个问题。 HELP  You say a, one, two, several, etc. billionwithout a final ‘s’ on ‘billion’. Billions (of…)can be used if there is no number or quantity before it.
