use your ˈhead(BrE alsouse your ˈloaf)
(informal)used to tell sb to think about sth, especially when they have asked for your opinion or said sth stupid你动动脑子；你仔细想想‘Why don't you want to see him again?’ ‘Oh, use your head!’“你为什么不愿再见到他？”“唔，你好好想想吧！”From rhyming slang, in whichloaf of breadstands for ‘head’.源自同韵俚语 loaf of bread，代表 head 的意思 。
