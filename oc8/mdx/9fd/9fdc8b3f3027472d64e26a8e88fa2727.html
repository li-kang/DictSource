nag / næɡ ; NAmE næɡ / verb , noun nag nags nagged nagging verb ( -gg- ) 1 [intransitive ,  transitive ] to keep complaining to sb about their behaviour or keep asking them to do sth 唠叨；不停地抱怨 SYN pester nag(at sb) ◆ Stop nagging—I'll do it as soon as I can. 别唠叨了，我会尽快做的。 nagsb (to do sth) ◆ She had been nagging him to paint the fence. 她一直唠叨，要他把围栏油漆一下。 2 [intransitive ,  transitive ] to worry or irritate you continuously 不断困扰；老使人烦恼 nagat sb ◆ A feeling of unease nagged at her. 一种不安的感觉一直困扰着她。 nagsb ◆ Doubts nagged me all evening. 我一晚上都没有摆脱心中的疑虑。 noun ( old-fashioned, informal) a horse 马 nag / næɡ ; NAmE næɡ /
