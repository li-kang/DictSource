the Feeding of the Five ˈThousand / ; NAmE / noun [singular ] a situation in which a lot of people need to be given food 众人求食的场面 ◆ I made breakfast for all my son's friends—it was like the Feeding of the Five Thousand. 我为儿子的所有朋友做了早餐，就像供应食物给五千人一样。 ORIGIN From the Bible story in which Jesus is said to have fed 5 000 people with five loaves of bread and two fish. 源自《圣经》中耶稣用五个饼和两条鱼让 5 000 人吃饱的故事。 Feeding of the Five ˈThousand / ; NAmE /
