the ˌcupboard is ˈbare
(BrE)used to say that there is no money for sth食橱是空的（指没钱购买）They are seeking more funds but the cupboard is bare.他们正在寻求更多的资金，然而根本找不到。This expression refers to a children's nursery rhyme about Old Mother Hubbard, who had nothing in her cupboard to feed her dog.源自一首关于老妈哈伯德的儿歌。她的食橱里没有喂狗的东西。
