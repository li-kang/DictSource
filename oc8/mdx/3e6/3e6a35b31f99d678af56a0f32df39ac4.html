reck·on·ing / ˈrekənɪŋ ; NAmE ˈrekənɪŋ / noun 1 [uncountable ,  countable ] the act of calculating sth, especially in a way that is not very exact 估计；估算；计算 ◆ By my reckoningyou still owe me £5. 我算计着，你还欠我 5 英镑。 2 [countable ,  usually singular ,  uncountable ] a time when sb's actions will be judged to be right or wrong and they may be punished 最后审判日；算总账 ◆ In the final reckoningtruth is rewarded. 在最后算总账的时候，真实情况最沾光。 ◆ Officials concerned with environmental policy predict that a day of reckoningwill come. 关心环境政策的官员们预言总有一天人们会受到报应。 IDIOM in/into/out of the ˈreckoning ( especially BrE) (especially in sport 尤用于体育运动 ) among/not among those who are likely to win or be successful 有（或没有）获胜的可能 reckoning reckonings reck·on·ing / ˈrekənɪŋ ; NAmE ˈrekənɪŋ /
