sus·ten·ance AWL / ˈsʌstənəns ; NAmE ˈsʌstənəns / noun [uncountable ] ( formal) 1 the food and drink that people, animals and plants need to live and stay healthy 食物；营养；养料 ◆ There's not much sustenance in a bowl of soup. 一碗汤没多少营养。 ◆ ( figurative) Arguing would only give further sustenance to his allegations. 越是争论，他越会觉得自己那些说法有道理。 2 sustenance(of sth) the process of making sth continue to exist 维持；保持 ◆ Elections are essential for the sustenance of parliamentary democracy. 选举制度是维持议会民主所必不可少的。 sus·ten·ance / ˈsʌstənəns ; NAmE ˈsʌstənəns /
