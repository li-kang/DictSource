(alsoˌstrike sb/sth ˈfrom sth)to remove sb/sth's name from sth, such as the list of members of a professional group把某人（或某事物）除名Strike her name off the list.把她的名字从名单上删掉。The doctor was struck off(= not allowed to continue to work as a doctor)for incompetence.那名医生因不称职而遭取消了执业资格。
