preen / priːn ; NAmE priːn / verb 1 [transitive ,  intransitive ] preen(yourself) ( usually disapproving) to spend a lot of time making yourself look attractive and then admiring your appearance 刻意打扮，精心修饰（并自我欣赏） ◆ Will you stop preening yourself in front of the mirror? 你别对着镜子打扮个没完行不行？ 2 [transitive ] preenyourself (on sth) ( usually disapproving) to feel very pleased with yourself about sth and show other people how pleased you are 顾盼自雄；沾沾自喜；得意扬扬 3 [intransitive ,  transitive ] preen(itself) (of a bird 鸟 ) to clean itself or make its feathers smooth with its beak （用喙）整理羽毛 preen preens preened preening preen / priːn ; NAmE priːn /
