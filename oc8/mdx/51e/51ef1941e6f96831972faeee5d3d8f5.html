sub·side / səbˈsaɪd ; NAmE səbˈsaɪd / verb 1 [intransitive ] to become calmer or quieter 趋于平静；平息；减弱；消退 ◆ She waited nervously for his anger to subside. 她提心吊胆地等他的怒气平息下来。 ◆ I took an aspirin and the pain gradually subsided. 我服了一片阿司匹林，疼痛逐渐缓解了。 2 [intransitive ] (of water 水 ) to go back to a normal level 回落；减退 ◆ The flood waters gradually subsided. 洪水缓缓回落。 3 [intransitive ] (of land or a building 地面或建筑物 ) to sink to a lower level; to sink lower into the ground 下沉；沉降；下陷 ◆ Weak foundations caused the house to subside. 由于地基不实，房子出现下陷。 subside subsides subsided subsiding sub·side / səbˈsaɪd ; NAmE səbˈsaɪd /
