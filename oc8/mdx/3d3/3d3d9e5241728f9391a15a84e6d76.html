no ˈproblem(informal)1(alsonot a ˈproblem)used to show that you are happy to help sb or that sth will be easy to do（表示乐于相助或事情容易做）没问题‘Can I pay by credit card?’ ‘Yes, no problem.’“我能用信用卡付款吗？”“行，没问题。”2used after sb has thanked you or said they are sorry for sth（回答别人的道谢或道歉）没什么，不客气，没关系‘Thanks for the ride.’ ‘No problem.’“谢谢你载我一程。”“不客气。”
