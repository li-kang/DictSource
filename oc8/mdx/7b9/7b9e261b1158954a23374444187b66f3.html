if you ˈlike(informal)1used to politely agree to sth or to suggest sth（礼貌地同意或建议）如果你要这样做，你要是愿意的话‘Shall we stop now?’ ‘If you like.’“我们现在停下来好吗？”“听你的。”If you like, we could go out this evening.你如果愿意的话，咱们今晚可以出去。2used when you express sth in a new way or when you are not confident about sth（用新方式表达或不确定时说）换句话说，可以说It was, if you like, the dawn of a new era.换句话说，那就是新时代的黎明。
