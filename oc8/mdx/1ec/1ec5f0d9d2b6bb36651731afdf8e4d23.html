fore·hand / ˈfɔːhænd ; NAmE ˈfɔːrhænd / noun [usually singular ] (in tennis,etc. 网球等 ) a way of hitting a ball in which the inner part of the hand (= the palm) faces the ball as it is hit 正手击球；正手；正拍 ◆ She has a strong forehand. 她正手击球强劲有力。 ◆ a forehand volley 正手截击球 ◆ He served to his opponent's forehand. 他把球发向对方的正手方向。 ☞compare backhand forehand forehands fore·hand / ˈfɔːhænd ; NAmE ˈfɔːrhænd /
