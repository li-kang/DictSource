lan·guish / ˈlæŋɡwɪʃ ; NAmE ˈlæŋɡwɪʃ / verb ( formal) 1 [intransitive ] languish(in sth) to be forced to stay somewhere or suffer sth unpleasant for a long time 被迫滞留；长期受苦；受煎熬 ◆ She continues to languish in a foreign prison. 她继续被囚禁在一所外国的监狱里。 2 [intransitive ] to become weaker or fail to make progress 变得衰弱；未能取得进展 ◆ The share price languished at 102p. 股票价格停滞在 102 便士上。 languish languishes languished languishing lan·guish / ˈlæŋɡwɪʃ ; NAmE ˈlæŋɡwɪʃ /
