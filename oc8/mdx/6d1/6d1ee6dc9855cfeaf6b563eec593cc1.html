un·awares / ˌʌnəˈweəz ; NAmE ˌʌnəˈwerz / adverb 1 when not expected 猝然；出其不意地；冷不防 ◆ The camera had caught her unawares. 她毫无防备地被拍摄下来。 ◆ The announcement took me unawares. 这项声明令我感到意外。 ◆ She came upon him unawares when he was searching her room. 他在翻她屋子时，冷不防被她撞见了。 2 ( formal) without noticing or realizing 不留神地；未注意到；不知不觉地 ◆ He slipped unawares into sleep. 他不知不觉地睡着了。 un·awares / ˌʌnəˈweəz ; NAmE ˌʌnəˈwerz /
