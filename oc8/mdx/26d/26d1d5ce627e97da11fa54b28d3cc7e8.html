taker / ˈteɪkə(r) ; NAmE ˈteɪkər / noun 1 [usually plural ] a person who is willing to accept sth that is being offered 接受者；收受人 ◆ They won't find many takers for the house at that price. 以这样的要价，他们不会为房子找到多少承让人的。 2 ( often in compounds 常构成复合词 ) a person who takes sth 接受者 ◆ drug takers 吸毒者 ◆ It is better to be a giver than a taker. 施比受有福。 taker takers taker / ˈteɪkə(r) ; NAmE ˈteɪkər /
