Illnessis used to talk about both more severe and more minor medical problems, and those that affect mental health. *disease 指较严重的身体疾病，尤其是影响身体器官的疾病。illness 指重病或小病均可，也可指精神上的疾病： ◆ heart/kidney/liver illness ◆ mental disease Diseaseis not used about a period of illness. *disease 不指患病期： ◆ she died after a long disease ■ disorder ( rather formal) an illness that causes a part of the body to stop functioning correctly 指失调、紊乱、不适、疾病： ◆ a rare disorder of the liver 一种罕见的肝病 NOTEA disorderis generally not infectious.
