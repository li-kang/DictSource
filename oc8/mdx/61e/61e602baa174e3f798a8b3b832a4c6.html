upper·most / ˈʌpəməʊst ; NAmE ˈʌpərmoʊst / adjective , adverb adjective 1 [usually before noun ] ( formal) higher or nearer the top than other things 最高的；最上端的；最上面的 ◆ the uppermost branches of the tree 树顶端的枝桠 2 [not usually before noun ] more important than other things in a particular situation 最重要；最关键 ◆ These thoughts were uppermost in my mind. 我心里想得最多的就是这些事。 adverb ( formal) in the highest position; facing upwards 处于最高位置；面向上地 ◆ Place the material on a flat surface, shiny side uppermost. 把材料放在平面上，有光的一面朝上。 upper·most / ˈʌpəməʊst ; NAmE ˈʌpərmoʊst /
