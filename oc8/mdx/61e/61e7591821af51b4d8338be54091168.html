suf·fer ★ / ˈsʌfə(r) ; NAmE ˈsʌfər / verb 1 ★ [intransitive ] to be badly affected by a disease, pain, sadness, a lack of sth, etc. （因疾病、痛苦、悲伤等）受苦，受难，受折磨 ◆ I hate to see animals suffering. 我不忍心看动物受苦。 sufferfrom sth ◆ He suffers from asthma. 他患有哮喘。 ◆ road accident victims suffering from shock 交通事故中受到惊吓的受害者 ◆ Many companies are suffering from a shortage of skilled staff. 许多公司苦于缺乏熟练员工。 sufferfor sth ◆ He made a rash decision and now he is suffering for it. 他当初草率决定，现在吃苦头了。 2 ★ [transitive ] suffersth to experience sth unpleasant, such as injury, defeat or loss 遭受；蒙受 ◆ He suffered a massive heart attack. 他的心脏病发作很严重。 ◆ The party suffered a humiliating defeat in the general election. 该党在大选中惨败。 ◆ The company suffered huge losses in the last financial year. 公司在上一财政年度出现巨额亏损。 3 ★ [intransitive ] to become worse 变差；变糟 ◆ His school work is suffering because of family problems. 由于家庭问题，他的学业日渐退步。 IDIOM not suffer fools ˈgladly to have very little patience with people that you think are stupid 不愿迁就笨人；不能容忍愚蠢者 suffer suffers suffered suffering suf·fer / ˈsʌfə(r) ; NAmE ˈsʌfər /
