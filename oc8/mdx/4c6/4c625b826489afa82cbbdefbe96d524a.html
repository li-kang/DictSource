come/crawl out of the ˈwoodwork
(informal,disapproving)if you say that sbcomes/crawls out of the woodwork,you mean that they have suddenly appeared in order to express an opinion or to take advantage of a situation突然露面；纷纷出笼When he won the lottery, all sorts of distant relatives came out of the woodwork.他博彩中奖后，八杆子打不着的亲戚都突然来登门造访。
