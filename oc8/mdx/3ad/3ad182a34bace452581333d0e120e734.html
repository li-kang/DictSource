Would you care for sth?|Would you care to do sth?
(formal)used to ask sb politely if they would like sth or would like to do sth, or if they would be willing to do sth（礼貌问语）您想要，您喜欢，您愿意Would you care for another drink?您再来一杯好吗？If you'd care to follow me, I'll show you where his office is.如果您愿意跟我走，我会把您领到他的办公室去。note atwant
