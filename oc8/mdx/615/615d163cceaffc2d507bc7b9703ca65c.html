mono·tone / ˈmɒnətəʊn ; NAmE ˈmɑːnətoʊn / noun , adjective monotone monotones noun [singular ] a dull sound or way of speaking in which the tone and volume remain the same and therefore seem boring 单调；单调的声音 ◆ He spoke in a flat monotone. 他说话单调低沉。 adjective [only before noun ] without any changes or differences in sound or colour （声音或色彩）单调的 ◆ He spoke in a monotone drawl. 他用慢吞吞又单调的语气说话。 ◆ monotone engravings 单调的版画 mono·tone / ˈmɒnətəʊn ; NAmE ˈmɑːnətoʊn /
