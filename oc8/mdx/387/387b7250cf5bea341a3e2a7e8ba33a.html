spe·cial·ly ★ / ˈspeʃəli ; NAmE ˈspeʃəli / adverb 1 ★ for a particular purpose, person, etc. 专门地；特意 ◆ The ring was specially made for her. 这枚戒指是为她订做的。 ◆ a specially designed diet plan 专门制订的饮食方案 ◆ We came specially to see you. 我们特意来看你。 2 ( informal) more than usual or more than other things 格外；特别；尤其 ◆ It will be hard to work today—specially when it's so warm and sunny outside. 今天无心工作，尤其是外面这样风和日丽。 ◆ I hate homework.
