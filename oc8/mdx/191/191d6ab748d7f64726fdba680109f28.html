ˈbask in sth
to enjoy the good feelings that you have when other people praise or admire you, or when they give you a lot of attention沉浸，沐浴（在赞美、关注等中）He had always basked in his parents' attention.他一直沉浸在父母的呵护中。I never mindedbasking inmy wife'sreflected glory(= enjoying the praise, attention, etc. she got).妻子的荣耀惠及于我，我并不觉得有什么不好意思。
