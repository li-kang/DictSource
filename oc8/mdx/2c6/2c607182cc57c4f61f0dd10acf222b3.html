adˈhere to sth
(formal)to behave according to a particular law, rule, set of instructions, etc; to follow a particular set of beliefs or a fixed way of doing sth坚持，遵守，遵循（法律、规章、指示、信念等）For ten months he adhered to a strict no-fat low-salt diet.十个月来他严格坚持无脂肪少盐饮食。She adheres to teaching methods she learned over 30 years ago.她依循她 30 多年前所学的教学法教学。
