abroad ★ / əˈbrɔːd ; NAmE əˈbrɔːd / adverb ( especially BrE) 1 ★ in or to a foreign country 在国外；到国外 ◆ to be/go/travel/live abroad 在外国；出国；到国外旅行；在外国居住 ◆ She worked abroad for a year. 她在国外工作了一年。 ◆ imports of cheap food from abroad 国外廉价食物的进口 ◆ He was famous, both at home and abroad (= in his own country and in other countries). 他享誉国内外。 2 ( formal) being talked about or felt by many people 广为流传 ◆ There was news abroad that a change was coming. 盛传即将有一个变动。 3 ( old use) outside; outdoors 在室外；到室外；户外 abroad / əˈbrɔːd ; NAmE əˈbrɔːd /
