braai / braɪ ; NAmE braɪ / noun , verb ( SAfrE) noun 1 ( also braai·vleis ) a social event at which food is cooked outdoors over an open fire 露天烧烤餐会 ◆ We're having a braai at our place next Saturday. 下个星期六我们家要搞一场露天烧烤。 ◆ a bring-and-braai (= everyone brings their own meat) 自带肉食的露天烧烤餐会 2 the surface or piece of equipment where the fire is made 烧烤位；烧烤炉 verb ( braais , braai·ing or braaing , braaied ) [transitive ,  intransitive ] braai(sth) to cook food over an open fire, especially as part of a social event 露天烧烤（聚会） ☞compare barbecue braai / braɪ ; NAmE braɪ /
