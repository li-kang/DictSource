are extremely happy. *cry 泛指因悲伤、痛苦或喜悦而流泪。 ■ To sobmeans to cry noisily, taking sudden, sharp breaths. *sob 意为啜泣、抽噎。 ■ To wailmeans to cry in a loud high voice. *wail 意为嚎啕大哭。 ■ To whimpermeans to cry making low, weak noises. *whimper 意为低声啜泣、呜咽。 ■ To weep( literaryor formal) means to cry quietly for a long time. *weep （文学或正式用语）意为饮泣。 All these verbs can be used like ‘say’. 以上动词均与 say 用法相同： ◆ ‘I
