im·pli·ca·ture / ˈɪmplɪkətʃə(r) ; NAmE ˈɪmplɪkətʃər / noun ( technical 术语 ) 1 [uncountable ] the act of suggesting that you feel or think sth is true, without saying so directly 含蓄行为；含蓄表达 2 [countable ] something that you can understand from what is said, but which is not stated directly 隐含意；言外之意 ◆ An implicature of ‘Some of my friends came’ is ‘Some of my friends did not come’. “我的一些朋友来了”这句话的言外之意是“我的一些朋友没有来”。 im·pli·ca·ture / ˈɪmplɪkətʃə(r) ; NAmE ˈɪmplɪkətʃər /
