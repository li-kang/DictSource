pare / peə(r) ; NAmE per / verb 1 to remove the thin outer layer of sth, especially of fruit 削皮，去皮（尤指果皮） paresth ◆ She pared the apple. 她削了苹果。 paresth from sth ◆ First, pare the rind from the lemon. 首先把柠檬皮剥掉。 paresth off/away ◆ He pared away the excess glue with a razor blade. 他用剃须刀片将溢胶刮去。 ☞see also paring knife 2 paresth (back/down) to gradually reduce the size or amount 逐步减小（数量或体积）；使缩小 ◆ The training budget has been pared back to a minimum. 培训预算已被削减到最低限度。 ◆ The workforce has been pared to the bone (= reduced to the lowest possible level). 公司员工已被裁减到极限。 3 paresth ( especially BrE) to cut away the edges of sth, especially your nails, in order to make them smooth and neat 修剪（指甲等） ☞see also parings pare pares pared paring pare / peə(r) ; NAmE per /
