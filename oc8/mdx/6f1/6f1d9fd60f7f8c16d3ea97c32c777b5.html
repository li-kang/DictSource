bums on ˈseats
(BrE) (informal)used to refer to the number of people who attend a show, talk, etc, especially when emphasizing the need or desire to attract a large number观众（或听众）的数量大（尤用于强调吸引大量观众的需要或愿望）They're not bothered about attracting the right audience—they just want bums on seats.他们不在乎观众是什么样的人，只求卖座。
