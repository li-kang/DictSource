pygmy ( also pigmy ) / ˈpɪɡmi ; NAmE ˈpɪɡmi / noun , adjective pygmy pygmies noun ( plural pygmies , pigmies ) 1 Pygmy a member of a race of very short people living in parts of Africa and SE Asia 俾格米人（生活于非洲和东南亚部份地区，身材矮小） 2 ( disapproving) a very small person or thing or one that is weak in some way 矮小的人（或物）；侏儒；弱小者 ◆ He regarded them as intellectual pygmies. 他把他们视为智力低弱的人。 adjective [only before noun ] used to describe a plant or species(= type) of animal that is much smaller than other similar kinds （比同类动植物）小得多的，矮小的，微小的 ◆ a pygmy shrew 倭鼩鼱 pygmy / ˈpɪɡmi ; NAmE ˈpɪɡmi /
