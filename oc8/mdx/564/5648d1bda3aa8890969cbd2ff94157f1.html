pat·ter / ˈpætə(r) ; NAmE ˈpætər / noun , verb patter patters pattered pattering noun 1 [singular ] the sound that is made by sth repeatedly hitting a surface quickly and lightly 吧嗒吧嗒的响声；急速的轻拍声 ◆ the patter of feet/footsteps 哒哒的脚步声 ◆ the patter of rain on the roof 雨打屋顶的啪哒声 2 [uncountable ,  singular ] fast continuous talk by sb who is trying to sell you sth or entertain you （为推销或娱乐的）不间断说话 ◆ sales patter 推销员的一口气说话 IDIOM the patter of tiny feet ( informal or humorous) a way of referring to children when sb wants, or is going to have, a baby （用于想要或即将有孩子时）小宝宝的脚步声 ◆ We can't wait to hear the patter of tiny feet. 我们恨不得早点有个小宝宝。 verb 1 [intransitive ] + adv./prep. to make quick, light sounds as a surface is being hit several times 发出轻快的拍打声 ◆ Rain pattered against the window. 雨点啪哒啪哒地敲击着窗子。 2 [intransitive ] + adv./prep. to walk with light steps in a particular direction 轻盈地走 ◆ I heard her feet pattering along the corridor. 我听到她步履轻盈地在走廊上走过。 pat·ter / ˈpætə(r) ; NAmE ˈpætər /
