Mary Celeste / ˌmeəri sɪˈlest ; NAmE ˌmeri sɪˈlest / ( also Marie Celeste ) noun [singular ] used to talk about a place where all the people who should be there have disappeared in a mysterious way 神秘的失踪地 ◆ Where is everyone? It's like the Mary Celeste here today. 人都到哪儿去了？今天这里的人都神秘失踪了。 ORIGIN From the name of the US ship Mary Celeste,which in 1872 was found at sea with nobody on board. 源自美国玛丽 · 赛勒斯特号轮船船名，1872 年在海上被发现时船上空无一人。 Mary Celeste / ˌmeəri sɪˈlest ; NAmE ˌmeri sɪˈlest /
