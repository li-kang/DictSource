in/under the ˈcircumstances
used before or after a statement to show that you have thought about the conditions that affect a situation before making a decision or a statement在这种情况下；既然如此Under the circumstances, it seemed better not to tell him about the accident.在这种情况下，不告诉他有关这次事故的情况似乎更好。She did the job very well in the circumstances.她在那种情况下仍把工作干得很出色。
