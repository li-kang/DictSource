Gon·dwana / ɡɒnˈdwɑːnə ; NAmE ɡɑːnˈdwɑːnə / ( also Gon·dwana·land / ɡɒnˈdwɑːnəlænd ; NAmE ɡɑːnˈdwɑːnəlænd / ) noun [singular ] ( geology 地 ) a very large area of land that existed in the southern hemispheremillions of years ago.
