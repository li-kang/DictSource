Jek·yll and Hyde / ˌdʒekl ən ˈhaɪd ; NAmE ˌdʒekl ən ˈhaɪd / noun [singular ] a person who is sometimes very pleasant ( Jekyll) and sometimes very unpleasant ( Hyde) or who leads two very separate lives 两重性格交替出现的人；具有善恶双重人格的人 ORIGIN From the story by Robert Louis Stevenson, Dr Jekyll and Mr Hyde,in which Dr Jekyll takes a drug which separates the good and bad sides of his personality into two characters. All the negative aspects go into the character of Mr Hyde. 源自罗伯特 · 路易斯 · 史蒂文森的小说《化身博士》，小说中哲基尔医生服用了一种药，把他性格中的善与恶分在两个人物身上，所有的恶念都分给了海德先生。 Jek·yll and Hyde / ˌdʒekl ən ˈhaɪd ; NAmE ˌdʒekl ən ˈhaɪd /
