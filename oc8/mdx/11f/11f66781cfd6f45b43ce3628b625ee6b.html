ˌswitch ˈoff
(informal)to stop thinking about sth or paying attention to sth不再想着；不再注意；失去兴趣When I hear the word ‘football’ I switch off(= because I am not interested in it).我听见“足球”两个字就腻味。The only time he really switches off(= stops thinking about work, etc.)is when we're on vacation.只有在我们外出度假的时候，他才真正无牵无挂。
