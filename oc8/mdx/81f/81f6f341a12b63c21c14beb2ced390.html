bal·let / ˈbæleɪ ; NAmE ˈbæleɪ / noun 1 [uncountable ] a style of dancing that tells a dramatic story with music but no talking or singing 芭蕾舞 ◆ She wants to be a ballet dancer. 她想当芭蕾舞演员。 ◆ ballet shoes 芭蕾舞鞋 2 [countable ] a story or work of art performed by a group of balletdancers 芭蕾舞剧 ◆ ‘Swan Lake’ is one of the great classical ballets. 《天鹅湖》是一部伟大的古典芭蕾舞剧。 3 [countable + singular or plural verb ] a group of dancers who work and perform ballettogether 芭蕾舞团 ◆ members of the Royal Ballet 皇家芭蕾舞团成员 ballet ballets bal·let / ˈbæleɪ ; NAmE ˈbæleɪ /
