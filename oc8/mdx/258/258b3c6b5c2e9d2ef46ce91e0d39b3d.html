post·modi·fier / ˌpəʊstˈmɒdɪfaɪə(r) ; NAmE ˌpoʊstˈmɑːdɪfaɪər / noun ( grammar 语法 ) a word or group of words that describes a noun phrase or restricts its meaning in some way, and is placed after it 后置修饰语；后修饰成分 ◆ In ‘the house on the corner’, ‘on the corner’ is a postmodifier. 在 the house on the corner 中，on the corner 是后置修饰语。 ☞compare modifier , premodifier post·modi·fier / ˌpəʊstˈmɒdɪfaɪə(r) ; NAmE ˌpoʊstˈmɑːdɪfaɪər /
