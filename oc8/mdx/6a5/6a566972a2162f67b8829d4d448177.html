(informal)used when you are not exactly sure about a person, thing or place（表示对人、事、地点不太有把握）He's a factory supervisor or something.他是工厂监督一类的人吧。‘Who said so?’ ‘Oh, somebody or other. I can't remember who it was.’“这是谁说的？”“啊，某一个人吧，我记不清是谁了。”
