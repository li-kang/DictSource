(= tickets that have been returned) 今晚的演出有退票吗？ 3 [uncountable ] the fact of making sth no longer valid 作废；废除；取消；中止 ◆ the cancellation of the contract 合同的取消 cancellation cancellations can·cel·la·tion / ˌkænsəˈleɪʃn ; NAmE ˌkænsəˈleɪʃn /
