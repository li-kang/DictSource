par·take / pɑːˈteɪk ; NAmE pɑːrˈteɪk / verb ( par·took / pɑːˈtʊk ; NAmE pɑːrˈtʊk / par·taken / pɑːˈteɪkən ; NAmE pɑːrˈteɪkən / ) ( formal) 1 [intransitive ] partake(of sth) ( old-fashioned or humorous) to eat or drink sth especially sth that is offered to you 吃，喝，享用（尤指给予的食物） ◆ Would you care to partake of some refreshment? 你想吃些东西吗？ 2 [intransitive ] partake(in sth) ( old-fashioned) to take part in an activity 参加；参与 ◆ They preferred not to partake in the social life of the town. 他们不想参加这个镇的社交活动。 PHRASAL VERB parˈtake of sth ( formal) to have some of a particular quality 具有（部份特性）；有点 ◆ His work partakes of the aesthetic fashions of his time. 他的作品具有当时的某些审美时尚。 partake partakes partook partaking partaken par·take / pɑːˈteɪk ; NAmE pɑːrˈteɪk / par·took / pɑːˈtʊk ; NAmE pɑːrˈtʊk / par·taken / pɑːˈteɪkən ; NAmE pɑːrˈteɪkən /
