ˌpoke aˈbout/aˈround
(informal)to look for sth, especially sth that is hidden among other things that you have to move搜查；翻找The police spent the day poking around in his office but found nothing.警察花了一天时间搜查他的办公室，但一无所获。(figurative)We've had journalists poking around and asking a lot of questions.我们给记者不断追问。
