re·cov·ery AWL / rɪˈkʌvəri ; NAmE rɪˈkʌvəri / noun ( plural re·cov·eries ) 1 [uncountable ,  countable ,  usually singular ] recovery(from sth) the process of becoming well again after an illness or injury 恢复；痊愈 ◆ My father has made afull recoveryfrom the operation. 我父亲手术后已完全康复了。 ◆ to make a remarkable/quick/speedy/slow, etc. recovery 恢复显著、很快、迅速、缓慢等 ◆ She is on the road to (= making progress towards) recovery. 她正在康复之中。 2 [uncountable ,  countable ,  usually singular ] recovery(in sth) the process of improving or becoming stronger again 改善；回升；复苏 ◆ The government is forecasting an economic recovery. 政府预测经济会复苏。 ◆ a recovery in consumer spending 消费支出的回升 ◆ The economy is showing signs of recovery. 经济呈现出复苏的迹象。 3 [uncountable ] recovery(of sth) the action or process of getting sth back that has been lost or stolen 取回；收回；复得 ◆ There is a reward for information leading to the recovery of the missing diamonds. 凡能为找回丢失的钻石提供线索者可获奖赏。 4 [uncountable ] ( also reˈcovery room [countable ] ) the room in a hospital where patients are kept immediately after an operation （供刚做完手术的病人使用的）监护室 recovery recoveries re·cov·ery / rɪˈkʌvəri ; NAmE rɪˈkʌvəri /
