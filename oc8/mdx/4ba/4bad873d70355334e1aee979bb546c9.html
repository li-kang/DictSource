neigh·bour·hood ★ ( especially US neigh·bor·hood ) / ˈneɪbəhʊd ; NAmE ˈneɪbərhʊd / noun 1 ★ a district or an area of a town; the people who live there 街区；城区；（统称）某街区（或城区）的居民 ◆ We grew up in the same neighbourhood. 我们是在同一条街上长大的。 ◆ a poor/quiet/residential neighbourhood 贫困的街区；安静的城区；住宅区 ◆ Manhattan is divided into distinct neighborhoods. 曼哈顿分为几个区，风格各异。 ◆ the neighbourhood police 社区警察 ◆ He shouted so loudly that the whole neighbourhood could hear him. 他叫得那么大声，整条街的人都能听到。 2 ★ the area that you are in or the area near a particular place 所在地；邻近的地方 SYN vicinity ◆ We searched the surrounding neighbourhood for the missing boy. 我们在附近寻找失踪的男孩儿。 ◆ Houses in the neighbourhood ofParis are extremely expensive. 巴黎附近一带的住房极其昂贵。 IDIOM in the neighbourhood of (of a number or an amount 数量 ) approximately; not exactly 大约；上下 ◆ It cost in the neighbourhood of $500. 这大约花费了 500 元。 neighbourhood neighbourhoods neigh·bour·hood / ˈneɪbəhʊd ; NAmE ˈneɪbərhʊd /
