afara / əˈfɑːrə ; NAmE əˈfɑːrə / ( also limba ) noun 1 [countable ] a tall tree that grows in W Africa （西非）阿法拉树，榄仁树 2 [uncountable ] the wood from this tree, often used for making furniture 阿法拉木，榄仁木（常用于制作家具） 3 [countable ] ( WAfrE) a bridge, usually made of wood 桥；（通常指）木桥 afara / əˈfɑːrə ; NAmE əˈfɑːrə /
