Achil·les heel / əˌkɪliːz ˈhiːl ; NAmE əˌkɪliːz ˈhiːl / noun [singular ] a weak point or fault in sb's character, which can be attacked by other people 致命弱点；致命的缺陷 ORIGIN Named after the Greek hero Achilles.When he was a small child, his mother held him below the surface of the river Styx to protect him against any injury. She held him by his heel, which therefore was not touched by the water. Achilles died after being wounded by an arrow in the heel. 源自希腊英雄阿喀琉斯（Achilles）的故事。传说他年幼时，母亲把他浸在斯提克斯冥河中，使他刀枪不入。由于母亲提着他的脚踵，因此他的脚踵没有沾上河水。后来他因脚踵中箭身亡。 Achilles heel Achilles heels Achil·les heel / əˌkɪliːz ˈhiːl ; NAmE əˌkɪliːz ˈhiːl /
