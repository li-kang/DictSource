but then (again)1however; on the other hand然而；另一方面He might agree. But then again he might have a completely different opinion.他可能同意，但也可能会意见完全相反。2used before a statement that explains or gives a reason for what has just been said（引出解释或原因）She speaks very good Italian. But then she did live in Rome for a year(= so it's not surprising).她的意大利语讲得很流利。不过她毕竟在罗马生活过一年。
