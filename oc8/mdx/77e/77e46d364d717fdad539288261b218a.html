bob·ble / ˈbɒbl ; NAmE ˈbɑːbl / noun , verb bobble bobbles noun ( BrE) a small, soft ball, usually made of wool, that is used especially for decorating clothes 小绒球，小羊毛球（尤用作衣服缀饰） SYN pompom ◆ a woolly hat with a bobble on top 帽顶带一只小绒球的羊毛帽子 verb ( informal) 1 [intransitive ] + adv./prep. to move along the ground with small bounces 弹跳着向前移动 ◆ The ball somehow bobbled into the net. 球不知怎么在地上弹了几下钻入网窝。 2 [transitive ] bobblesth ( NAmE) to drop a ball or to fail to stop it 漏球；漏接球；没有停住球 ◆ She tried to catch the ball but bobbled it. 她想要接球，但没有接住。 3 [intransitive ] ( BrE) ( informal) (of a piece of clothing, especially one made of wool 衣服，尤指毛料的 ) to become covered in very small balls of fibre 起绒线球 bob·ble / ˈbɒbl ; NAmE ˈbɑːbl /
